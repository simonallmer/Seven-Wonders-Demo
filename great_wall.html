Great Wall Rules

GOAL
Reduce the enemy army below four warriors. Fallen warriors never return.

PLACE
Deploy a warrior from reserve onto a field within two clear steps of your supply chain — the chain runs from your Legendary Field ✦ through friendly warriors.

MOVE
Step up to two fields in any direction, then choose a new facing. Standing still to rotate is allowed.

SHOOT
Fire an arrow along your facing. It fells the first vulnerable warrior and chain-fires onward in the victim's facing; shields reflect head-on shots and deflect angled ones. Friendly fire is real.

SPIN
Strike all eight surrounding fields at once, then choose a new facing. Shields still protect.

SHIELD
Each warrior's shield guards its three rear directions — the back and both rear flanks. Attacks from anywhere else are lethal.

SUPPLY
If an enemy stands on your Legendary Field ✦, your supply is cut: no reinforcements until it is removed.
