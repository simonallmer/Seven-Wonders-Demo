Skyscraper Rules

The Objective: Dominate the skyscraper by either claiming over half of the territory (83 fields) or having more stones than your opponent when moves are exhausted.

Connection: You can only place a stone if it has a clear line of sight through the 3D grid to another stone of your color.

Shadow Rule: Opponent windows block your light. You cannot project connections through enemy-occupied spaces.

Capture: Forming a complete line of 5 windows (horizontal) or 7 windows (vertical) triggers a capture. The player with the majority of stones in that line claims all fields in it.

Split Capture: If a move closes multiple lines simultaneously, each is evaluated for majority. In this case, the trigger stone you placed is safe from being captured by the opponent, provided it won one of the lines it completed.
