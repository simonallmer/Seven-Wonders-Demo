TEMPLE

White to move

Temple Rules

GOAL Bring one of your stones to the marked Artemis field on the opposite side of the board.

SETUP Each player's 9 stones are automatically placed in the triangle surrounding their own Artemis field — 3 lines of stones at the start.

GAMEPLAY White moves first. Players alternate turns. Each stone has two options:

1 — WALK Move the stone to an unoccupied adjacent field by following the path. A stone can only move forwards or sideways — never backwards.

2 — LEAP Leap over an adjacent stone to land on an unoccupied field beyond it. If you leap over an opponent's stone, that stone is removed. If you leap over a stone of your own color, nothing happens to it.
You may leap over more than one stone in a single turn, provided you always land on an unoccupied field in between. The direction may change by up to 45° after each landing. Stones cannot leap over 2 or more stones standing next to each other.
Leap of Faith: A leap may also carry a stone outside the Temple entirely — that stone is then removed from the game.

WINNING The first player to place a stone on the opposing Artemis field wins. If a threefold repetition occurs, the game ends in a draw. A player may also concede at any time by skipping their turn.
