KEYWORDS

A register of every movement, attack and action in the Seven Wonders series.
Each game is unique, yet many keywords carry across titles.

General

Name Description Game
Stone Game piece in four colors. Pyramid, Gardens, Temple, Mausoleum, Colossus, Pharos, Great Wall
Die A game piece whose value indicates the number of fields for movement. Statue
Reservoir Game pieces not yet on the field. A player may place one when wished. Statue, Great Wall

Movements

Name Description Game
Run A stone runs to any accessible field on the same or on lower levels. Pyramid, Colossus
Jump A stone jumps to an adjacent unoccupied field. Pyramid
Push An adjacent stone on the same level is pushed one field. Pyramid, Colossus
Stack Single stones of the same color stack to form a tower. Gardens
Trail Stones are moved with at least one stone from the bottom being placed per field. Gardens
Walk Movement equals one field. Temple
Leap Adjacent stones are leaped over if next field in line is free. Temple
Place Stone or die is placed on an unoccupied field from the reservoir (out of game). Statue, Great Wall
Stride Movement equals die. Statue
Glide A stone moves in one line until an object stops the movement. Mausoleum
Beam Stones in an unbroken horizontal or vertical line of sight may send one move to a stone of their color. Pharos
Light A field is lit up in the player's color, requiring an unbroken line of sight through the structure and no higher than the elevator. Skyscraper

Attacks & Actions

Name Description Game
Smash Stone from higher level falls on stone from lower level through a run. Smashed stone is removed. Pyramid
Fall Stone on the edge field of the lowest level is pushed out of the game field. Fallen stone is removed. Pyramid
Osiris Stones of the same color get surrounded by the opposing color on the same level and in one line. These stones get removed. Pyramid
Hades Stones of the same color get surrounded by the opposing color horizontally and vertically with no adjacent empty fields. These stones get removed. Colossus
Hit Stone or die of the opposing color enters an occupied field. Hit game piece is removed. Statue, Pharos
Diminish Lower die by 1 to force opponents invincible die to move. Statue
Circle A fully encircled stone is removed if the number of opponent's stones exceeds the number of player's stones. Mausoleum
Collapse A captured stone's field collapses into a permanent pit. The pit blocks movement and counts as a wall when encircling. Mausoleum
Trap If a stone has only 1 liberty left, it is unable to move. Mausoleum
Tilting If a stone ends its move on a direction field, the board tilts that way and all stones slide until blocked. A new direction field reached during the slide tilts the board again. Colossus
Row Completing a full sequence of 5 windows (horizontally or vertically) on the same block initiates a Row. The player holding the majority of illuminated fields within that sequence secures the entire line. Skyscraper
