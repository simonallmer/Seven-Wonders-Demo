MAUSOLEUM

The Mausoleum at Halicarnassus

Current Turn: White
White Stones: 9 • Black Stones: 9

Mausoleum Rules

GOAL Reduce the opponent's stones to fewer than four by forming circles. The first player to achieve this wins.

SETUP Each player receives 9 stones. They are automatically placed on the two shortest rows on each player's side of the board. The starting player is determined randomly.

GLIDE On your turn, move one stone in a straight line. It continues in that direction until it hits the edge of the board or a stone blocks its path — stopping on the space immediately before. No two stones may share a space.

CIRCLE A stone is fully encircled when it has no adjacent empty spaces. A fully encircled stone is removed if it is surrounded by more opponent stones than friendly stones. If a stone has only one free adjacent field remaining, it is trapped and unable to move.
Fewer stones are needed to complete a circle or trap along the edge of the board.
If two circles are formed in one turn, both are resolved simultaneously.

WINNING The game ends immediately when a player is reduced to fewer than 4 stones. That player loses. If both players fall below 4 stones on the same turn, the game is a draw.
