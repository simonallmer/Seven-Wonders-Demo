Mausoleum Rules

GOAL
Reduce opponent to fewer than 4 stones.

GLIDE
Move stones in straight lines across the network until blocked by another stone or the edge.

ENCIRCLE
Remove stones by encircling them with more of your stones than they have friendly neighbors.

TRAPPED
A stone with only one empty neighbor cannot move.
