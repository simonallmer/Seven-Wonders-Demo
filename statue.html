STATUE

The Statue of Zeus at Olympia

Player 1 (White) Turn
Reserve: P1 8 | P2 8 • Board: P1 0 | P2 0
Player 1's turn. Click a die to move, or choose an action.

Statue Rules

GOAL Reduce your opponent's dice on the board to fewer than three through attacks.

SETUP Both players receive 8 dice of their color. All dice begin in the Statue of Zeus reserve (off the board). White moves first.

GAMEPLAY On each turn, choose one of three actions:

1 — PLACE A DIE Place one die from your reserve onto any unoccupied field. Newly placed dice always start with a face value of 1.

2 — STRIDE WITH A DIE Move one of your dice horizontally or vertically a number of spaces equal to its face value. If it lands on an opponent's die, that die is removed — the moving die may continue its remaining movement and attack multiple opponents in one turn. After each move, the die's value increases by 1. Reaching 6 resets it back to 1.
Dice cannot move through or land on dice of the same color. Only one die per field.

3 — DIMINISH Reduce the face value of any of your dice that shows more than 1 by one pip. The opponent is then forced to move any of their dice currently showing a 1. This action can only be used if the opponent has dice showing 1 that are able to move.

WINNING The game ends immediately when a player is reduced to fewer than 3 dice on the board. That player loses.
