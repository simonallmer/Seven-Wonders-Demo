Great Wall — 4 Players

THE BOARD
Two Great Walls meet in a cross. Four armies hold the four arms — White (south), Red (west), Black (north), Blue (east) — and fight for the central crossing. You play White; the rest are computer-led.

GOAL
Drive every rival army below four warriors. Last army holding the wall wins.

PLACE
Deploy a warrior from reserve onto a field within two clear steps of your supply chain — the chain runs from your Legendary Field ✦ at your arm's tip through friendly warriors.

MOVE
Step up to two fields in any direction, then choose a new facing. Standing still to rotate is allowed.

SHOOT
Fire an arrow along your facing. It fells the first vulnerable warrior and chain-fires onward in the victim's facing; shields reflect head-on shots and deflect angled ones. Friendly fire is real — and so is felling a third army's warrior.

SPIN
Strike all eight surrounding fields at once, then choose a new facing. Shields still protect.

SHIELD
Each warrior's shield guards its three rear directions. Attacks from anywhere else are lethal.

SUPPLY
If an enemy stands on your Legendary Field ✦, your supply is cut: no reinforcements until it is retaken.
