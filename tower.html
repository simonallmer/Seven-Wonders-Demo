Tower Rules

GOAL
Control all 8 tiles on any single ring, OR two full columns top-to-bottom of the tower.

TILES
Each tile is a triangular prism: its top shows your color (ownership), one face shows its active number (1–3). You start with 12 in your pool.

PLACE
Raise a tile from your pool onto any empty slot — your color up, number 1 outward.

MOVE
Pick one of your tiles, then tap an adjacent empty slot (around a ring or up/down a column) to slide it there. It keeps its number and color.

ATTACK
No separate button — after picking your tile, tap an adjacent enemy chain instead of an empty slot. If your chain's numbers sum higher than theirs, you capture the whole enemy chain (flipped to your color, reset to 1). Green = you win, red = too strong.

LEVEL UP
Rotate one of your tiles to the next number (1→2→3→1). Cycling 3→1 rings the bell: immediately move any one tile (yours or the enemy's) to an adjacent empty slot.
