Pyramid Rules

GOAL
Conquer the Pyramid by bringing four of your stones to the victory fields on the top level.

SETUP
Seven stones per side are automatically placed at the base level, facing each other.

GAMEPLAY
White moves first. Each stone has three options:

1 — RUN
Move forward in a straight line to an empty field. Moving to a lower occupied field Smashes that stone.

2 — JUMP
Move to the adjacent empty field one level above.

3 — PUSH
Move into an adjacent stone, pushing it one step. A stone pushed off the corner Falls and is removed.

OSIRIS
If stones of one color are surrounded by opponent's stones in a line, those stones are removed.

WINNING
First to place 4 stones on victory fields wins.
