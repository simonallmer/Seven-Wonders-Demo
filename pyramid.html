The Great Pyramid of Giza

Pyramid Rules

GOAL Conquer the Pyramid by bringing four of your stones to the victory fields on the top level.

SETUP Seven stones per side are automatically placed at the base level, facing each other across the board.

GAMEPLAY White moves first. Players alternate turns. Each stone has three options:

1 — RUN Move forward in a straight line to an empty field on the same or a lower level. Cannot run past or share a field with another stone. Moving to a lower occupied field Smashes that stone (removes it). No further move is allowed after a Smash.

2 — JUMP Move to the adjacent empty field one level above.

3 — PUSH Move into an adjacent stone's field, pushing it one step further. A pushed stone can smash a stone on a lower level, but cannot push another stone on the same level. A stone pushed off a corner of the lowest level Falls and is removed. A pushed stone cannot push back on the very next turn.

OSIRIS If any number of stones of one color are surrounded by the opponent's stones in a single line on the same level, those stones are removed. A Double Osiris — where alternating stones of both colors are removed — is also possible.

WINNING The first player to place 4 stones on the marked victory fields wins. If both players have fewer than 4 stones remaining, or a threefold repetition occurs, the game ends in a draw.
